Character name

Izzie Stevens

Actor

Katherine Heigl

Bio

Izzie Stevens is known for her compassion, emotional intelligence, and unwavering dedication to her patients. After a difficult upbringing and working as a model to pay for medical school, she became one of the most empathetic and determined interns at Seattle Grace. Her journey includes major challenges, intense relationships, and one of the show's most dramatic medical storylines involving her cancer diagnosis.

-Strong emotional intuition -Exceptional patient connection
